Biography

My name is Steve Rogers and I was born in New York, New York. I applied to the United States Army during the height of WWII, but was denied because I was a very unathletic small, skinny, sick looking kid. However I caught the eye of Dr.Abrahan Erskine and was offered an oppurtunity to take part in an experiment called Operation Rebirth. This is where I was injected with the Super-Solider serum. This formula not only made me the peak of human effciency, but from there on out I would be Captian Rogers.
